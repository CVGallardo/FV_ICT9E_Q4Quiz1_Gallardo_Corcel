Coron Bay Islands, Palawan

In these northern islands of Palawan, a day can be ruined by other visitors. But, there are plenty of secluded spots where you can escape the crowds. This area is the ancestral land of the Tagbanua people and they have done a good job of preserving their homeland, with its green lakes, stunning cliffs and white sand beaches.

Chocolate Hills, Bohol

The Chocolate Hills constitute a geological feature in the Bohol province of the Philippines. Numbering at least 1,260 and possibly as many as 1,776 hills, they span over 50 square kilometers. Cloaked in green grass, these hills take on a brown hue in the dry season, giving rise to their name.
